1. Stafood

Stafood is a startup located only in France, in four cities: Lyon, Lille, Paris and Marseille. At Stafood, we propose an original concept of distributing a basket of the best products of the region at a small price. Our offer is aimed at restaurants but also people who like to cook. Our system of baskets is ecological, we propose fresh products including the ones that are difform and thus that can't be sold on the market.

2. About the creator

Daphne Stavrou is the chief of the project. She imagined the concept of the startup five years ago and her idea became real thanks to the support of people who shared the same opinion that fresh products should be available to all.
